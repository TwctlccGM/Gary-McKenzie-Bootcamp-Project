Bootcamp 2020: Forest scene

My Processing project is a 2D game which has utilised arrays, loops, randomness, if/else statements and multiple classes. The goal of the game is to defeat the enemy triangle before it defeats you and before your time runs out. I have bugtested it thoroughly and polished it with slight animations, responsive sounds and visual feedback. The sound of the enemy shooting and the animated background can be toggled on or off during gameplay depending on the player's preference. Controls are displayed on screen before gameplay begins.
